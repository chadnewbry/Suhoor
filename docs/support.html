Frequently Asked Questions

How are prayer times calculated?
Suhoor uses established astronomical algorithms from recognized Islamic organizations (ISNA, MWL, Egyptian General Authority, and more). You can change the calculation method in Settings → Prayer Time Configuration.
Why does the app need my location?
Location is used to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for your specific position. Location data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.
How do I change the calculation method?
Go to Settings → Prayer Time Configuration and select your preferred calculation method.
How do I manage my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions to manage or cancel.
How do I enable notifications?
Go to Settings → Notifications in the app to configure suhoor reminders, prayer time alerts, and other notifications.
Is my data backed up?
Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. iCloud sync is planned for a future update (v1.1).
