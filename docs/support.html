Common Questions

Prayer times seem wrong for my location.
Go to Settings → Prayer Times and try a different calculation method. Different methods are used in different regions.

How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases.

Notifications aren't working.
Make sure notifications are enabled in iOS Settings → Suhoor → Notifications.
