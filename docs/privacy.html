Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

Chad Newbry, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") built Suhoor as a Ramadan companion app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Suhoor is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Notification Preferences: Your notification settings are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults.
  • App Settings: Preferences such as calculation method, fasting settings, and display options are stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to premium features, transactions are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use analytics or tracking services.
  • We do not collect personal identifiers, usage data, or browsing history.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or ad tracking.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Data Storage

All app data (settings, hydration tracking, meal plans, Quran reading progress, sehri checklists) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Third-Party Services

Suhoor uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app subscriptions. Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Suhoor does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages as it does not collect personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Effective Date" above.
