Suhoor is the Ramadan companion you've been looking for — focused, beautiful, and built for the month that matters most.

Unlike bloated Islamic apps that try to do everything, Suhoor does one thing exceptionally well: it makes your Ramadan effortless. From sehri to iftar, every feature is designed to keep you focused on what matters.

LIVE COUNTDOWNS & PRAYER TIMES
Never miss sehri or iftar again. A live countdown shows exactly how long until your next meal, with accurate prayer times calculated for your location. Live Activities keep the countdown on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island — no need to open the app.

FASTING TRACKER WITH STREAKS
Track every fast through Ramadan with a simple, satisfying tracker. Build streaks, see your progress, and sync fasting data to Apple Health via HealthKit. Detailed analytics show your fasting patterns and achievements across the month.

QURAN READING SCHEDULE
Complete the Quran during Ramadan with a personalized reading plan. Track your daily progress through a structured khatam schedule that keeps you on pace throughout the holy month.

RAMADAN CALENDAR & ISLAMIC CALENDAR
A beautiful Ramadan calendar with all your sehri and iftar times at a glance. Plan your entire month with the traditional imsakiya view, including taraweeh times. Follow along with the Islamic calendar to stay connected to key dates.

SUHOOR MEAL & HYDRATION PLANNING
Plan your suhoor meals and track hydration to stay energized throughout your fast. Smart reminders help you prepare before sehar time so you never wake up unprepared.

DUAS & DAILY REMINDERS
Access essential Ramadan duas for sehri, iftar, and taraweeh. Notifications for prayer times, athan alerts, and fasting reminders keep you on track without effort.

WIDGETS & APPLE WATCH
Glanceable widgets for your Home Screen show countdown timers and prayer times. Apple Watch support keeps your fasting timer on your wrist. Whether you're at work or on the go, Suhoor stays with you.

QIBLA COMPASS WITH AR
Find the Qibla direction quickly using the built-in compass or point your camera for an augmented reality Qibla experience.

PRIVACY-FIRST, NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED
Suhoor is local-first with no accounts, no third-party analytics, and no data collection. Your Ramadan is yours alone.

SUHOOR PREMIUM
Unlock the full experience with Suhoor Premium. Pricing details and subscription terms are shown before purchase. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage subscriptions in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
